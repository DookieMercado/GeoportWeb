FEATURES OF THE APP

Packed with powerful tools to make road reporting faster, smarter, and more reliable.

Automated Geo Tagging
After capturing a photo, the app automatically detects your exact location upon AI verification, ensuring precise geo-tagging for faster emergency response and accurate incident mapping.
AI Report Identification
Advanced AI technology analyzes your photos to automatically identify road hazards such as potholes, road cracks, and vehicular accidents, streamlining the reporting process.
Smart Rerouting
Intelligent navigation system that automatically suggests alternate routes based on realtime incident reports, helping you avoid traffic and road hazards.
Admin Conference Call
The app includes a secure in app calling feature that enables CDRRMO to directly reach out to users for urgent report verification and necessary follow-ups.
Identity Verification
Secure user verification system ensures all reports come from authenticated residents, maintaining data integrity and preventing false reports.
Community Alerts
Real-time notifications system that keeps the community informed about road conditions, ongoing incidents, and important traffic updates in your area.

ABOUT GEOPORT

Geoport Malaybalay is a community-powered mobile app designed to improve road safety and response efficiency. It enables verified users to report road defects such as potholes, cracks, and vehicular accidents (VA/crashes) using their smartphone camera and location services.

By focusing on these critical incidents, the app ensures that all reports are valid, geo-tagged, and helpful for both commuters and responders. Through AI-assisted detection, smart rerouting, and admin verification, Geoport connects the community and authorities to respond faster, reduce risks, and maintain safer roads across Malaybalay City.
